Coptic App

Overview

The Coptic App is a mobile application designed to serve the Coptic Christian community with features such as daily prayers, hymns, church calendar, and educational content. It aims to facilitate spiritual growth and community engagement by providing users with access to a wide range of religious resources and tools directly from their smartphones.

Role and Responsibilities

As the lead developer for the Coptic App, my responsibilities included overseeing the design and development of the application, implementing core functionalities, and ensuring a smooth user experience. I worked on both frontend and backend development, integrated various features such as prayer notifications, hymn lyrics, and calendar events, and collaborated with the design team to create an intuitive and visually appealing interface.

Key Technologies

  • Frontend: React Native
  • Backend: Node.js, Express
  • Database: MongoDB
  • Others: Firebase, RESTful APIs, Push Notifications

Features

  • Daily Prayers: Access to daily prayers and religious texts.
  • Hymns: A collection of hymns with lyrics and audio.
  • Church Calendar: Up-to-date church events and liturgical calendar.
  • Educational Content: Articles, sermons, and resources for spiritual education.
  • Push Notifications: Timely reminders for prayers, events, and updates.

Challenges

Content Management: Ensuring accurate and up-to-date content required a robust content management system and regular updates.

Performance Optimization: Optimizing app performance and load times while handling rich media content was a key challenge.

Outcome

  • Enhanced Accessibility: The app provided users with easy access to religious resources and community updates from anywhere.
  • Positive User Feedback: Received positive feedback for the app's comprehensive features and user-friendly design.
  • Increased Engagement: The app saw a significant increase in user engagement and daily active users.
