Faisal Islamic Bank of Egypt Website

Overview

Faisal Islamic Bank of Egypt's website is a comprehensive online platform providing extensive information and services related to Islamic banking and finance. The site features an overview of the bank's history, mission, and values, emphasizing its commitment to Sharia-compliant banking practices. Users can access details about various personal and business banking products, including accounts, investments, financing, and e-banking services. The website also offers insights into the bank's social responsibility initiatives, financial reports, and latest news updates. Additionally, it provides customer support through various channels, ensuring a user-friendly and informative experience for visitors.

Role and Responsibilities

As the Senior Software Engineer, I was responsible for overseeing both the frontend and backend development of the website. I worked closely with the design team to create a modern, responsive interface and collaborated with the backend team to ensure secure and efficient data handling. My tasks included setting up the Umbraco CMS, developing custom modules, integrating third-party APIs, and ensuring compliance with banking security standards.

Key Technologies

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Backend: ASP.NET, SiteCore
  • Database: Microsoft SQL Server
  • Others: RESTful APIs, OAuth2

Features

  • Faisal Online: Faisal Online Service reflects an understanding of your busy lifestyle by offering you an easy, secure, fast tool of accessing your Faisal Online Service reflects an understanding of your busy life.
  • Faisal Cash: Faisal Cash Wallet provides you with a wide range of services that suit a more fast-paced lifestyle to facilitate your daily transactions and avoid the risks of carrying money.
  • Loan Applications: Easy-to-use loan application forms with real-time status updates.
  • Interactive Financial Tools: Calculators for loan payments, savings, and investment planning.
  • Branch Locator: Interactive map to find nearby branches and ATMs.

Challenges

Security Compliance: Ensuring the website meets stringent banking security standards and regulations was a significant challenge. I implemented multi-factor authentication, data encryption, and secure API integration to address these requirements.

Data Integration: Integrating with various banking systems and ensuring real-time data synchronization required careful planning and robust error handling mechanisms.

Outcome

  • Increased User Engagement: The website saw a 60% increase in user engagement within the first six months of launch.
  • Positive Feedback: Received commendations from customers and stakeholders for the intuitive design and comprehensive features.
  • Performance Optimization: Achieved a 35% reduction in page load times through performance optimizations.
